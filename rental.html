Our Studio

Our studio is a spacious, professionally designed environment equipped with state-of-the-art facilities to support both training and creative production.

We feature premium ballet barres, sprung flooring, and professional Marley surfaces to ensure safety and performance quality.

The space includes male and female changing areas and is ideal for private lessons, rehearsals, photo and video shoots.

It can also be transformed into a performance venue with wings, lighting, and seating available upon request.

On-site parking is available.
